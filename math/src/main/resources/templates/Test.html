Austria in German (Österreich)

officially the Republic of Austria , The Capital is Vienna (Wien). Austria is a landlocked country of over 8.8 million people in Central Europe. The territory of Austria covers 83,879 km2 (32,386 sq mi) Austria map. The terrain is highly mountainous,lying within the Alps, only 32% of the country is below 500 m (1,640 ft), and its highest point is 3,798 m (12,461 ft). official Language is German It has eight federal states.
